WolfSheep — Account Deletion & Data Removal

Developed by Shutak / WolfSheep.fun. Here is everything you need to know about deleting your account and data.

📋 How to Request Account Deletion

1 Sign In to Your Account

Go to the Profile page and sign in with your Google account or username/password. You must be authenticated to request deletion — this ensures that only you can delete your own data.

2 Submit a Deletion Request

Use the form at the bottom of this page to submit a deletion request. Optionally tell us why you are leaving — your feedback helps us improve the game.

3 Send an Email (Alternative Method)

If you cannot sign in (e.g., you lost access to your account), send an email to shutakswm@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request — WolfSheep". Include as much identifying information as possible:

  • Your in-game nickname
  • Your approximate ELO rating
  • The email address associated with your Google account (if you used Google sign-in)
  • The approximate date you created the account

4 Confirmation & Processing

Once we receive your request, we will verify your identity and process the deletion within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email (if an email is associated with your account).

📊 What Data Is Affected

Data Type Action Details
Account credentials 🗑 Deleted Username, hashed password, Google ID link removed
Nickname 🗑 Deleted Custom display name removed
Email address 🗑 Deleted Google email association removed
Profile picture 🗑 Deleted Google avatar URL removed
Game statistics 🗑 Deleted Wins, losses, ELO rating, match history removed
Leaderboard data 🗑 Deleted Your entry removed from leaderboard immediately
Match results ✅ Preserved (anonymized) Past game outcomes against other players remain for ELO integrity, but your identity is removed
Chat messages / emotes 🗑 Deleted In-game emotes are ephemeral and not stored

⏱ Data Retention Periods

  • Active accounts: Data is retained as long as your account exists.
  • After deletion request: Account is removed from the database within 7 business days.
  • Server logs: Connection timestamps and IP addresses are automatically rotated and purged within 30 days.
  • Analytics (Umami): Fully anonymized, no personal data is collected. No cookies are used.
