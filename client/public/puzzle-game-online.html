🧩 A Puzzle Game That Sharpens Your Mind

WolfSheep isn't just another puzzle game online — it's a competitive strategic puzzle where every move matters. On a 9×9 grid, you must decide: move forward, jump over your opponent, or place one of your 10 walls to reshape the board. Each turn is a fresh tactical problem to solve.

Inspired by the award-winning Quoridor, this board puzzle game adds asymmetric roles: as the Wolf, you hunt and corner your prey. As the Sheep, you navigate a maze of walls to reach freedom. Two completely different puzzle-solving mindsets in one game.

🧠 Why WolfSheep Is the Ultimate Online Puzzle Game

  • Move, jump, or wall — a puzzle every turn: Three actions, infinite combinations. Anticipate your opponent's strategy and counter it.
  • Grid-based spatial reasoning: Visualize paths, calculate distances, predict wall placements. Like a chess puzzle, but with a dynamic board.
  • Asymmetric puzzle solving: Wolf thinks like a hunter (closing distance, cutting escapes). Sheep thinks like an escape artist (finding gaps, baiting walls).
  • 10 walls = endless tactics: Each wall changes the topology of the board. Master the art of path-blocking and maze creation.
  • Time pressure adds intensity: Solve puzzles against the clock — 1+5 blitz for fast thinking, 5 min classic for deep analysis.
