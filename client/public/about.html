ABOUT US

The story behind WolfSheep — a free online strategy board game

🐺 What is WolfSheep?

WolfSheep is a free online strategy board game inspired by the classic Quoridor. Two players face off on a 9×9 grid: the Wolf must catch the opponent, while the Sheep must break through to the far side. Each player has 10 walls to block paths and outmaneuver their rival.

Think of it as a strategy game on a grid where every move matters. You can play with a friend online, challenge strangers worldwide, or practice against our AI bot. No downloads, no ads, no paywalls — just pure tactical gameplay in your browser.

🔄 How WolfSheep Differs from Classic Quoridor

WolfSheep is heavily inspired by Quoridor but introduces unique twists that make it a fresh experience:

  • Asymmetric roles: Unlike Quoridor where both players have the same goal, WolfSheep gives each player a distinct objective — the Wolf hunts, the Sheep escapes. This creates deeper strategic variety.
  • Online matchmaking: Built-in matchmaking with ELO rating means you always find a fair opponent. No need to set up a physical board or coordinate schedules.
  • Three time controls: Blitz (1+5), Rapid (3+2), and Classic (5 min) — adapt the pace to your style. Quoridor doesn't have a clock.
  • Neon visual theme: A sleek cyberpunk aesthetic with glowing pieces, particle effects, and smooth animations — a modern take on the classic wooden board.
  • AI bot training: Practice against the computer anytime. Improve your wall placement tactics and jump strategies without the pressure of a human opponent.

🎯 Our Mission

We built WolfSheep to bring the thrill of strategic board gaming to everyone — for free, with no downloads, no ads, and no paywalls. Just pure intellectual combat in a stunning neon arena. We believe great games should be accessible: open your browser, click play, and you're in.

👨‍💻 Developer

WolfSheep is developed and maintained by an indie developer passionate about strategy games and clean design. The game is a labor of love, built from scratch with modern web technologies. Every feature — from the real-time multiplayer to the ELO rating system — was handcrafted to deliver the best possible experience.

🛠 Technology Stack

Frontend: Vanilla JavaScript, HTML5 Canvas, CSS3
Backend: Node.js, Express, Socket.IO
Storage: File-based JSON database
Real-time: WebSocket communication for instant multiplayer
