The story behind WolfSheep

🐺 What is WolfSheep?

WolfSheep is a free online strategy board game inspired by the classic Quoridor. Two players face off on a 9×9 grid: the Wolf must catch the opponent, while the Sheep must break through to the far side. Each player has 10 walls to block paths and outmaneuver their rival.

🎯 Our Mission

We built WolfSheep to bring the thrill of strategic board gaming to everyone — for free, with no downloads, no ads, and no paywalls. Just pure intellectual combat in a stunning neon arena.

👨‍💻 Developer

WolfSheep is developed and maintained by an indie developer passionate about strategy games and clean design. The game is a labor of love, built from scratch with modern web technologies.

🛠 Technology Stack

Frontend: Vanilla JavaScript, HTML5 Canvas, CSS3
Backend: Node.js, Express, Socket.IO
Storage: File-based JSON database
Real-time: WebSocket communication for instant multiplayer
