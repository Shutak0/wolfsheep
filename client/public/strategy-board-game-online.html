🧠 The Best Strategy Board Game Online — Right in Your Browser

Looking for a strategy board game online that challenges your mind without cluttering your device? WolfSheep is a free browser board game that combines the tactical depth of chess-like wall placement with fast-paced one-on-one duels. No downloads, no ads, no paywalls — just pure strategic thinking.

Inspired by the acclaimed Quoridor, WolfSheep adds asymmetric roles (Wolf vs Sheep), three time controls, ELO rating, and a stunning neon visual theme. Whether you want to play with a friend online, challenge random opponents, or train against our AI bot — it's all here.

🎮 Why WolfSheep Is the Perfect Online Strategy Game

  • 9×9 grid strategy — Every cell matters. Plan your path, block your opponent, anticipate their next move.
  • 10 walls per player — Walls are your most powerful tool. Place them wisely to control the board.
  • Asymmetric objectives — Wolf must catch, Sheep must escape. Two completely different playstyles in one game.
  • Real-time multiplayer — Instant matchmaking finds you a fair opponent based on your ELO rating.
  • AI bot training — Practice wall placement, jumping, and path-finding against the computer.
  • Quick to learn, hard to master — Understand the rules in 30 seconds, spend months perfecting your strategy.

🌐 A Browser Board Game That Works Everywhere

WolfSheep runs on any modern browser — Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge — on desktop, tablet, or phone. The game adapts to your screen size, so you get a great experience whether you're at your desk or on the go. No installations, no app stores, no updates to worry about.
